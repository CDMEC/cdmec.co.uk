As a condition of the club's lease with the London Borough of Waltham Forest on whose land the railway is constructed, we operate the track for public running every Sunday Afternoon between April and the end of September.
Warning! Steam trains can emit hot dirt/oil/sparks which can mark clothes or burn skin. Every effort has been made to prevent this with the use of spark arresters, however it cannot be 100% mitigated and passengers travel at their own risk.
